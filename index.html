I am a Computer Science and Engineering graduate from BRAC University, with a passion for technology and education. I'm also proficient in using Microsoft Office tools for various tasks.

My enthusiasm for teaching has been a significant part of my life since 2018. Over the years, I've had the privilege of working with students at different educational levels, from SSC and HSC to O Levels and A Levels. Teaching has always been a source of joy and fulfillment for me, as I believe in the power of education to shape a brighter future.

Through this experience, I witnessed the transformative effect of collective action and the potential for change when like-minded individuals come together.

My journey continues, fueled by a desire to create a better world through technology, education, and community engagement. I am excited to embark on new adventures and collaborations that align with my values and aspirations.
